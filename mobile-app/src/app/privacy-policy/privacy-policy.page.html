Sama Pet Card Privacy Policy

At Sama, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the personal information of Sama Pet Card holders.

Information Collection

When you sign up for a Sama Pet Card, we collect the following personal information:

  • Full name
  • Mailing address
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Pet information (name, species, breed)

We may also collect information about your transactions and interactions when you use your Sama Pet Card at participating businesses.

Use of Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide you with your Sama Pet Card and associated discounts and benefits
  • Communicate with you about your account and the Sama Pet Card program
  • Analyze usage data to improve the program
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

  • Participating businesses to facilitate discount redemption
  • Service providers who assist us in operating the Sama Pet Card program
  • Law enforcement or other government entities as required by law

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, and misuse. This includes encryption, access controls, and monitoring.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, and delete your personal information held by Sama. You may also opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us.

To exercise your rights or for any other privacy-related inquiries, please contact our privacy team at:

- Email: privacy sama.com

- Phone: 1-800-555-1234

Changes to this Policy

Sama reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website.

By using the Sama Pet Card, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions or concerns, please let us know.
