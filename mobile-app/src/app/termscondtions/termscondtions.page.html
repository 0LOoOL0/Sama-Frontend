1. Overview

The Sama Pet Card is a loyalty program that provides exclusive discounts and benefits to cardholders at participating pet stores, pet services, pet accommodations, and other pet-related businesses. By using the Sama Pet Card, cardholders can save money on purchases, reservations, and services for their pets.

2. Eligibility

The Sama Pet Card is available to any individual who registers for the program. There is no cost to obtain a Sama Pet Card.

3. Earning Discounts

Cardholders can receive discounts at participating businesses by presenting their Sama Pet Card at the time of purchase or reservation. Discount amounts will vary by business, but will typically range from 5-20% off regular prices.

The following types of businesses currently participate in the Sama Pet Card program:

  • Pet stores
  • Veterinary clinics
  • Groomers
  • Boarding kennels
  • Pet daycares
  • Pet-friendly hotels and vacation rentals

A full list of participating businesses and their associated discounts can be found on the Sama Pet Card website.

4. Card Usage

The Sama Pet Card is non-transferable and can only be used by the individual to whom it is registered. Cards have no cash value and cannot be redeemed for cash.

Cardholders must present their physical Sama Pet Card at the time of purchase or reservation to receive discounts. Digital versions of the card are not accepted.

5. Termination of Program

Sama reserves the right to modify or terminate the Sama Pet Card program at any time. In the event of program termination, all outstanding discounts and benefits will be honored for 90 days after the announced end date.
